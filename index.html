Artifact 6: Aegisly Clinical Knowledge Assistant (RAG Capstone)

Title

Aegisly: A Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Clinical Knowledge Assistant for Dietary Safety

Introduction

This capstone project was developed during the Indiana Wesleyan University AI Residency and enhanced as a take-home project. Aegisly is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) healthcare assistant designed to improve dietary safety for individuals managing chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and chronic kidney disease. The system retrieves trusted nutritional and clinical information before generating responses, helping healthcare professionals and patients make safer food purchasing decisions.

Description

The artifact includes a production-oriented RAG architecture, a working chatbot, and a presentation describing the business problem, AI solution, product integration, data architecture, validation strategy, and value proposition. Unlike traditional chatbots that rely solely on model memory, Aegisly grounds every response using retrieved knowledge from trusted healthcare and nutrition sources.

Objective

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how Retrieval-Augmented Generation can improve the accuracy, transparency, and trustworthiness of AI systems operating in healthcare. The project focuses on reducing unsafe food purchases by providing grounded dietary recommendations before products are purchased online.

Process

Our team first developed a functional RAG prototype during the AI Residency Hackathon before expanding it into a production-ready capstone solution. My contributions included helping define the AI architecture, strengthening the technical justification, refining the product integration strategy, designing the validation approach, and documenting how retrieval, grounding, and guardrails improve chatbot reliability. The final solution emphasizes explainability, responsible AI, and practical deployment considerations.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • ChatGPT Custom GPT
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
  • Vector Search
  • OpenAI
  • PowerPoint
  • USDA FoodData Central
  • Open Food Facts
  • GitHub Pages

Value Proposition

Aegisly demonstrates how generative AI can safely support healthcare decision-making when combined with retrieval mechanisms and trusted external knowledge. The project illustrates how AI can reduce hallucinations, improve explainability, and provide clinically grounded recommendations that patients and healthcare professionals can trust.

Unique Value

Unlike traditional conversational AI systems, Aegisly retrieves information from verified healthcare knowledge sources before generating responses. This retrieval-first approach increases transparency, reduces misinformation, and provides traceable evidence supporting every recommendation whenever available.

Relevance

Retrieval-Augmented Generation is becoming one of the most widely adopted architectures for enterprise AI systems because it combines the reasoning ability of large language models with trustworthy external knowledge. This artifact demonstrates practical experience designing production-oriented AI systems that emphasize accuracy, validation, and responsible AI principles.
